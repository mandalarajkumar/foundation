ROBOTECH LAB - SATURDAY, 14TH APRIL 2017

Encouraging children's interest in science and technology, as well as increasing their technological literacy, may be regarded as one of the educational paradigms of this century. Robotics, with its multi-disciplinary approach, provides a vehicle for guiding primary and secondary school children toward an effective understanding of programming and engineering principles. Children find robotics stimulating and motivating, and that their interest in, and focus on, 'making the robot do what I want' leads them 'via the back door' to learn about programming and engineering in a way that is both well-grounded and comprehensive.

Course Description

A 10 days' course with emphasis on building and programming your own Robot. In this course, you will be programming an industrial grade microcontrollers to control and direct real-life robots rather than only using software for simulating robots on a computer.
